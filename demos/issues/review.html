Browser Developer Tools for Non-Developers

Discovering issues in web products

  1. The Issue tool shows you what problems and errors are in the current document.
  2. This allows you to report detailed issues to developers and site maintainers.
  3. It is important to filter the issues to avoid false positives.

Benefits:

  1. Detailed Issue reporting.

Issues:

  1. Lack of explanations what the issues mean and how to fix them.
  2. Lots of noise.
