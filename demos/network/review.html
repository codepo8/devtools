Browser Developer Tools for Non-Developers

Review: The Network tool

The Network tool allows you to:

  1. Access detailed information on data coming to and being sent from your computer.
  2. Simulate flaky and slow connections.
  3. Get easy access to images and videos on the web.

Benefits:

  1. Detailed information about all data flow.
  2. Direct access to web media.

Issues:

  1. Lots of information and options.
